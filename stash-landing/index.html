Your sessions

Why Stash?

Boost your focus, cut RAM usage, and keep your attention organized.

Switch context instantly

Jump between projects, downtime, or an urgent meeting — stash everything that's open into a box in one tap, so nothing breaks your focus.

Real app icons

Stash Desktop detects the apps on your machine, pulls their real icons, and reopens them right where you left off.

Restore & re-stash anytime

Reopen a session, or hit "Close back" to stash the same set again instantly — no need to rebuild it.

Fits every part of your day

Ways to unlock your time and attention.

Developers

Deep in a debug session and a meeting pops up? Stash VS Code, Docker, Discord and your Stack Overflow tabs in one go — restore later and pick up right where you left off. RAM stays free.

Writers & creators

Juggling projects? Keep "Writing" and "Video editing" as separate sessions and swap between them in one click — one focused task at a time.

Morning routine

Tired of opening calendar, email, notes and news one by one every morning? Save a "Start the day" session once — one button opens them all tomorrow.

Three simple steps

Nothing to learn — one button does it all.

  • Hit "Stash" — collect every open tab and app into one session, then close them automatically.
  • Focus fresh — a clear screen and a clear mind, fully on the task in front of you.
  • Hit "Restore" — whenever you're ready, one click brings everything back exactly as it was.

How to install

Up and running in three easy steps.

1

Download the installer

Click below to pick the version for your system — the installer downloads right away.

2

Bypass Windows SmartScreen

If a blue warning appears when you open the installer, click "More info" then "Run anyway" — the app just isn't code-signed yet, but the source is fully open and verifiable.

3

Use it safely

Stash is open source (AGPL-3.0). All your data stays on your machine — nothing is ever sent to the cloud.
